The Suite

What Is Oracle Fusion Cloud EPM?

Oracle Fusion Cloud Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) is a suite of nine interconnected cloud applications that covers the complete financial management lifecycle — from long-range strategic planning to statutory consolidation to tax reporting to disclosure. It is purpose-built for organisations that need to plan, close, and report with speed, accuracy, and auditability at enterprise scale.

The suite addresses three fundamental finance questions. What should happen? — answered by Planning, Profitability and Cost Management, and Freeform through budgets, forecasts, cost allocations, and scenario models. What did happen? — answered by Financial Consolidation and Close, Account Reconciliation, and Tax Reporting through the close process, consolidated group results, and corporate tax provision. How do we govern and communicate it? — answered by Enterprise Data Management, the EPM Platform, and Narrative Reporting through master data governance, shared infrastructure, and published disclosures.

Oracle's 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant position — Leader in both Financial Planning Software and Financial Close and Consolidation Solutions — reflects the depth of the suite. Nearly 10,000 organisations worldwide run Oracle EPM for mission-critical financial processes. Understanding how the nine modules connect is the difference between an EPM configurator and an EPM architect.
