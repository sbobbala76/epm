🗺️ The Six-Step Lifecycle
How all eight halls connect in a real GlobalMerge implementation

Every FCCS implementation follows the same sequence — whether it takes six weeks or six months. This lab compresses that lifecycle into six decision-rich steps, each anchored to the halls you have already walked. The order is not arbitrary: each step creates the foundation the next step depends on.

What Hall 8× is not

This is not a Hall 5 deep-dive on rules, a Hall 7 deep-dive on reporting, or a Hall 8 deep-dive on security. Those halls cover each domain in full. Hall 8× is the connective tissue — the implementation sequence that shows how every domain decision affects the next.
